voxunravel

A reordering of a voxel.js world generator created by following the blocks in a spiral pattern and placing them in a straight line

W to move forward

A to strafe left

D to strafe right

S to move backwards

Space to move up

Shift to move down

Move mouse to look around

F to start falling (you probably don't want to do this)

Double-click screen to start (you must allow full screen)
